Calorie Intake Guidelines

To Increase Caloric Intake (Goal: Gain Weight):

Consume Nutrient-Dense Foods

Focus on foods that are rich in calories and nutrients, such as nuts, seeds, nut butter, avocados, dried fruits, whole milk, cheese, and lean meats.

Eat More Frequently

Spread meals and snacks throughout the day to increase overall calorie intake. Including snacks between meals can be an effective way to add extra calories without feeling too full.

To Decrease Caloric Intake (Goal: Lose Weight):

Focus on Whole Foods

Prioritize whole, minimally processed foods that are high in fiber and nutrients. These foods tend to be more filling and can help reduce overall calorie intake.

Portion Control

Learn about appropriate portion sizes and use smaller plates and bowls to control portion sizes naturally.
